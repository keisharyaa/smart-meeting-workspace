# Decision Log

## DEC-001: Single-User MVP

The MVP has one authenticated workspace owner. PICs are lightweight contacts without login accounts.

## DEC-002: Four Action Workflow Statuses

Action statuses are To Do, In Progress, Blocked, and Done. Overdue is a deadline condition.

## DEC-003: Human Review Is Mandatory

AI output is always a draft and cannot become official without Approve & Publish.

## DEC-004: Combined Meeting Sources

A meeting may use multiple uploaded files and pasted text together.

## DEC-005: Final Technical Stack

Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui, Supabase Database/Auth/Storage, Gemini API, Vercel, and GitHub.
